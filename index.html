More About Us

Ultimax Building Solutions, Inc. has a comprehensive range of skilled workers (carpenters, mason, uPVC and aluminum fabricators, gypsum installers, etc.) housed in our own 6,000 sqm facility in Meycauayan, Bulacan. Our Company’s extensive manufacturing capability enables quality for our products to be strictly maintained thus allowing maximum ﬂexibility to meet individual project requirements.
